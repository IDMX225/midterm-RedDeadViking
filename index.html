About Me

Hi, my name is Nicholas Niva or Nick for short and I am currently a student at Raritan Valley Community College where I am studying Information Systems and Technology. The midterm for my Website Development and Design Class consisted of creating a website so I decided mine would be on something I love. One of the things I love most is Travel so I decided I would make a Travel Blog going through my experiences traveling all over the world. Below, take a look at some of the places I've been so far and some totally real reviews from some totally real people.

Adventures

Exploring India for a Summer

Spent a Summer in India with my Aunt and Sister and got to explore some beautiful history and landmarks everywhere from the Northern border with Pakistan to the more centrally located rural city of Udaipur.

Traveling Across Southern Europe

Traveled solo along the Mediterranean coast meeting up with friends and family on occasion and got to see everything from the historical beauty of Rome, Italy to the colorful and thriving city of Barcelona in Spain.

Enjoying the Nature and Culture of Puerto Rico

Spent a week checking out Puerto Rico and saw the amazing culture and food of San Juan along with the beautiful jungles, beaches, and bioluminescent bays across the island.
